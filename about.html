About Me


About Me | Unresume | Future

"It is the power of the mind to be unconquerable." - Seneca


I'm Meira D., I'm a writer in training. I try to write every day, whether it be code, tanka, haiku, freeverse, prose, or a random thought in my journal. I'm a avid reader of Thoreau, Poe, London, and all things paranormal. I also started a temporary wave of woodstock drawings on hospital dry erase boards across the state. Along with such characters as Snoopy, Scooby Doo, Muriel, and Courage the Cowardly Dog.

In the past, I've attended college to study Anthropology and draw. Right now, I cannot afford to attend. So I spend my days at the hospital visiting my brother and getting creative with paper and glitter. I'm also working on my first YA paranormal novella and pounding on the keys to compose poetry for publication in the future. Not to mention attempting to turn my lifestyle completely around with a round of experiments and learning to program.

Unresume

2005 Trekked across Germany and Poland for a month.

2008 Won NaNoWriMo noveling contest. Started learning to code.

2010 Earned a certificate in Social Innovation from the World Bank Association. Won a poetry constest by the author of the Blue Bloods vampire books.

2011-2013 Attended St.Charles Community College. I took courses in Anthropology, Web Coding, and Drawing. Joined CERT (Citizen Emergency Response Team). Joined PTK (Phi Theta Kappa) honor's society. Joined Github & began coding my own website. Began writing a novella.Taking on javascript. Busting out crazy lifestyle changes and hopefully taking the world by storm with some of my poetry.

In the future. ...

In the coming years, I hope to attend Code Fellows Bootcamp in Seattle. Learn Jade, Coffeescript, Node.js, python, jquery, and Javascript. I'm hoping to become more self reliant and am gathering materials to build wind turbines from scratch. I also hope to become fluent in French, travel through Canada, and start up a daily yoga practice. Not to mention become a writer, I already write, It's just time to make it more than that.

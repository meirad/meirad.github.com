In Brief

I'm Meira Datiya, (born in St.Charles, MO in Feb 1989). Grew up in Phoenix, Sedona, Flagstaff, and various places in New Mexico and California. Moved back to MO in 2000. Leaving for WA this summer.

When I was young, I skipped kindergarten, dropped out of 2nd & 6th grade and never looked back. I'm a self learner. I learned to code in HTML, CSS, and work with CMS's while tweaking blogs on wordpress, livejournal, vox, blogger, tumblr, and geocities.

I learned to knit on a dare. Helped a friend follow his dream in starting an autism village, and earned a certificate in social innovation.

Since then, I've been writing, learning, attending college, and enjoying life with my rescued border collie Oreo.

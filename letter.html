A Day In My Life.

Inspired by Gwen, I thought I'd share a day in my life. ...

I moved to Missouri in 2000, after living in the desert of Phoenix with minor trips to Flagstaff and New Mexico. Since then I've been learning about coding, minimalism, and stoicism while writing poetry and letters. I will be leaving the midwest in a month to travel northwest to the beautiful lands of Washington state.

I wake up, when I feel rested. Whether it's 7am or noon. I walk to the kitchen and boil water for tea. Then locate Oreo and attempt to wake her up to go outside. Some mornings we have tea on the front porch and listen to the birds chirp.

When we come back in I usually skip breakfast, if i'm hungry, I might bake some english muffins or make crepes. Then I log onto my laptop. I open my text editor, textastic, and post my writings online via github. I check my email, MIT's, write in my journal, and download articles to read later into Pocket. If i'm feeling lazy, I'll log off the internet and read on my ereader or study. I walk Oreo several times a day between mine and my parents house. Usually I have lunch and dinner at their house. In the afternoon, I do some basic Yoga. Water plants, and meditate.

In the evening I might watch something on netflix, read the articles i downloaded earlier, or listen to a podcast like "We're Alive". Falling asleep to the sounds of silence. ...

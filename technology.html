I don't do email anymore. There are a few exceptions to that rule. But mostly it goes like this. You wanna contact me? Use gpg and shoot me a message or Bitmessage me. I'll tell you now, I'm more likely to bitmessage you back instead of email you.

Why?

What if I don't have Bitmessage?

Gaman

I've been thinking a lot about the word Gaman lately. Not just because of Gwen's gaman challenge. But because of how things have changed lately for me. I now have a job and it's a challenge, not just because of what I do, but because I don't have a lot of time left for myself. I need some Gaman mojo.

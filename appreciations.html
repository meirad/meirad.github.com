Appreciations

Words

  • The Machine Stops by E.M. Forster Written in 1909 and still an important read for everyone in our time.
  • Voluntary Simplicity The book aligns with my inner values and gives idea's on how to continue pursuing them.
  • The Practice of the Wild This is one of those strange books you either love or hate. I happen to love it for it's descriptions of the wild.
  • A Guide to the Good Life: The Ancient Art of Stoic Joy by William B. Irvine.
  • Darling Magazine : The only magazine I read. I get the digital copies.

Peeps

  • Gwen Bell : Programmer && Writer. Keeps me on top of what's going on and from getting too used to my stack.
  • Ev Bogue : Programmer && Writer. Keeps me on top of crypto and computers.

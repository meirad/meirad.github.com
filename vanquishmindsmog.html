For years I have been searching for tranquility. A way to break through the distractions and disturbances that plague our daily lives.To vanquish the mindsmog.

If you can imagine a glinting sword thrust into your hands, so that you can battle for your sanity. I hope this is it.

But what is mindsmog?

Mindsmog is a collection of evils. I think it might be different for everyone. But there are a lot of things that are universal. Stress, anxiety, lashing out at others, confusion, disarray, and unhealthy choices are all indicators of mindsmog. If your are sitting in a room worrying about something and someone you love asks you something or reassures you and you don't hear a word they said. That's mindsmog.

It leaches joy from everyday moments by making sure you are not really there to appreciate them; even if you are in the same room. It taints everything it touches, like a virus. Sometimes, it even takes from you, like a thief it sneeks in and ransacks your core.

It can make you believe that you have to have to work those extra 4 hours a day on top of 12. Or that you can never be on enough commitee's. The world would launch Armageddon if you stepped away and took a few moments for youself. Hell, the entire universe could implode.

So, what can you do? Is there anything you can do? Grab your glinting sword. You're about to become a warrior.

About Me


About Me | Unresume | Future

'Everything is blooming most recklessly; if it were voices instead of colors, there would be an unbelievable shrieking into the heart of the night.' ~ Rilke


I'm Meira D., I'm a writer in training. I try to write every day, whether it be code, tanka, haiku, freeverse, prose, or a random thought in my journal. I'm a avid reader of Thoreau, Poe, London, and all things paranormal. I also started a temporary wave of woodstock drawings on hospital dry erase boards across the state. Along with such characters as Snoopy, Scooby Doo, Muriel, and Courage the Cowardly Dog.

In the past, I've attended college to study Anthropology and to draw. Right now, I can't afford to attend. So I spend my days at the hospital visiting my brother and getting creative with paper and glitter. I'm also working on my first YA paranormal novel and pounding on the keys to compose poetry for publication in the future. Not to mention attempting to turn my lifestyle completely around with a round of experiments and learning to program.

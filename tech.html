Why

A lot of you are probably wondering why I want to leave Apple & Google products behind. Why I'm leaving OSX for Arch. It's simple. If you are using fancy software that let's you work magic at the click of a button. You are being censored. You are locked out from the bones of the code and you can't really do anything on your own, without a corporation holding your hand.

Also, if you are pulled along by a rushing current of tech apps and trends that keep you addicted to something, you aren't living. You are a corporate zombie.

Simplifying your tech to the bare minimum leaves you time to live an un-zombified uncensored life, and that's what we all need.
